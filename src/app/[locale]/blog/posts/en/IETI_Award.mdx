## Academic Excellence Award  

On April 8, 2019, John Bryan Vergara was honored with the **Academic Excellence Award** at the International Electronics and Technical Institute Inc. This award highlights his dedication and outstanding performance during his Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Information and Communications Technology (ICT) track.  

This recognition reflects not only academic success but also commitment to excellence in technical education.
