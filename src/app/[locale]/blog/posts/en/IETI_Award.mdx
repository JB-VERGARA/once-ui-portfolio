## Skill of the Year 2025: Generative AI

Generative Artificial Intelligence (GenAI) has emerged as the **Skill of the Year 2025**, reshaping how businesses, professionals, and institutions innovate and operate. This recognition reflects the growing demand for practical, ethical, and strategic expertise in GenAI technologies.

## Academic Learning Sessions on Generative AI

The Generative AI program is structured into two comprehensive sessions, designed to build both foundational knowledge and advanced, real-world application skills.

---

## Session 1: Foundations and Business Integration of GenAI

Session 1 introduces the core concepts and practical applications of Generative AI, focusing on its role in modern workplaces and businesses.

### Key Topics Covered
- Introduction to Generative AI: principles, potential, and pitfalls  
- Overview of GenAI tools for the workplace  
- Prompt engineering for Large Language Models (LLMs)  
- Implementing Generative AI in business environments  
- The potential impact of Generative AI on businesses
