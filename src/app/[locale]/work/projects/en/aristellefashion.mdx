## Overview

Aristelle Fashion is a fashion business founded by Niña Mae Ramirez and John Bryan B. Vergara. We specialize in high-quality, handcrafted bracelets that are designed to be timeless, elegant, and versatile. Our mission is to provide our customers with unique, customizable pieces that complement their personal style.

Our product line features a wide range of bracelets, made from carefully selected materials and crafted with attention to detail. Whether you're looking for a statement piece or something subtle, Aristelle Fashion offers a collection for every occasion.

## Key Features

- **Product Showcase**: A clean and intuitive layout to display products and services, ensuring easy navigation and clear presentation.
- **Responsive Design**: Ensured seamless viewing on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
- **Dynamic Content**: Integrated with a backend powered by Contentful to handle product updates.
- **SEO Optimization**: Improved search engine visibility with best practices in Next.js and tailored strategies for the business's niche.
- **Direct-to-Messenger**: Provides users with an option to initiate conversations with the brand directly through messaging platforms.

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js**: A framework for building server-rendered and static web applications.
- **TypeScript**: Added type safety and improved developer experience.
- **Node.js**: Handled backend operations for API integrations and dynamic content updates.
- **Contentful**: A headless CMS used for content management, enabling easy updates to product listings and descriptions.

## Outcome

The website successfully highlights Aristelle Fashion's offerings, providing users with a seamless and engaging shopping experience while establishing the brand's online presence. It is designed to meet the needs of a modern audience, helping to enhance brand recognition and drive customer interactions. This project has contributed to increased user engagement and has strengthened Aristelle Fashion's position in the competitive market. It is accessible at [https://aristelle-fashion.vercel.app/](https://aristelle-fashion.vercel.app/)
